New Chinese-Only Content: This information applies only to the New Chinese version of Terraria.

The new Chinese version (新版中国大) of Terraria was developed by Xinxin Network, shortly after the shutting down of the old Chinese version. It was launched on September 28, 2021 for most phones, and October 9, 2021 for iOS.

Similar to its predecessor, the new Chinese version features many changes to the game and new content, however, all of it different from that of the old version. Aside from this, it runs the same desktop equivalent as the rest of the  Mobile version.
