The Underground Jungle is a dense network of small caverns, composed primarily of Mud, pools of Water, Thorny Bushes, and Beehives. It is located below the Surface of the Jungle biome and extends through to the Cavern layer.

The Underground Jungle always contains the Jungle Temple and 3 to 7 Bee Hives (depending on world size).

Common treasure rooms are Jungle Shrines, Underground Cabins, and Living Mahogany Trees.

In Hardmode, all enemies have a chance of dropping a Jungle Key () / Jungle Key Mold ().

Furthermore, the Underground Jungle undergoes several minor changes throughout the course of Hardmode:

  • Upon entering Hardmode, it becomes prone to being converted by the Evil Biome and starts spawning Chlorophyte Ore and Life Fruit.
  • After defeating one mechanical boss, Life Fruit begin spawning / spawn at a faster rate.
  • Once all three mechanical bosses have been defeated, Plantera's Bulbs begin spawning.

  • Desktop 1.2:
    • Added bosses Queen Bee and Hardmode Plantera and Golem.

    • Added Life Fruit (Hardmode), each permanently increases the player's health capacity by 5.
    • Added drops Bezoar and Hardmode Adhesive Bandage.
    • Hornets and Man Eaters can now drop Ancient Cobalt armor pieces.
    • Added Lac Beetle (Violet Husk), Spiked Jungle Slime, and Hardmode Jungle Creeper, Moss Hornet (Tattered Bee Wing), and Moth (Butterfly Dust).
    • Added Rich Mahogany, Sky Blue Flower, and Hardmode Angry Trapper (Uzi), Arapaima, and Giant Tortoise (Turtle Shell) to Jungle and Underground Jungle.
  • Desktop 1.0.6:
    • More gems spawn in the Underground Jungle.
    • Thorny Bushes growing from Jungle grass are now destroyed on contact with the player.
    • Underground Jungle now has Mud Background to avoid spawning Underground/Cavern layer enemies.
    • Added Jungle Spores.
  • Desktop 1.0.5:
    • Now has a unique track that plays both in the Underground Jungle and surface Jungle.
    • Hornets and Man Eaters no longer drop Jungle armor.
    • Doctor Bones now exclusive to Jungle and Underground Jungle.
    • Added Moonglow to Jungle and Underground Jungle.
  • Desktop 1.0.4:
    • Now found directly under the surface Jungle biome.
    • Jungle plants now grow underwater.
    • Added Jungle Bat and Piranha to Jungle and Underground Jungle.
  • Desktop 1.0.3: Hornets and Man Eaters can now drop Jungle armor pieces instead of Cobalt armor (same stats, re-named armor).
  • Desktop-Release: Introduced with Jungle Shrines, Jungle Grass Seeds, Jungle Rose, Hornet (Stinger), Man Eater (Vine) (both dropped original Cobalt armor).
